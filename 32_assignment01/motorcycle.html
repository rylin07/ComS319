Kawasaki Motorcycle Catalogue

Ninja 400:

    Ninja 400: A lightweight, nimble machine perfect for new riders and experienced alike, the Ninja 400 offers a perfect blend of sporty performance and everyday versatility with its 399cc twin-cylinder engine.

Ninja 500:

  • Ninja 500: Known for its reliability and efficiency, the Ninja 500 is a practical sportbike with a 498cc parallel-twin engine, offering a smooth power delivery ideal for both commuting and spirited riding.

Ninja 650:

  • Ninja 650: A middleweight motorcycle that balances sporty performance with everyday usability, the Ninja 650 features a 649cc parallel-twin engine and a comfortable riding position, making it great for both short rides and long journeys.

ZX-6R:

  • ZX-6R: A formidable player in the middleweight sportbike category, the ZX-6R is equipped with a 636cc inline-four engine, delivering sharp handling and high-revving performance, suited for the racetrack and aggressive street riding.

ZX-10R:

  • ZX-10R: A superbike with championship-winning pedigree, the ZX-10R boasts a powerful 998cc inline-four engine, advanced electronics, and aerodynamic design, designed to excel on both the racetrack and the open road.

ZX-14R:

  • ZX-14R: The ultimate sport-touring motorcycle, the ZX-14R combines incredible power with comfort and versatility. Its 1441cc inline-four engine offers staggering acceleration and top speed, making it a favorite for long-distance rides and straight-line performance.
